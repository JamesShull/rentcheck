Background

rentcheck.ninja give estimates of rental performance. By entering just a few numbers and making many assumptions, the performance can be quickly estimated. The assumptions can be edited on a per rental basis or set for all rentals with the menu.

Tips & Tricks

    Usage

  1. Confirm and save defaults for your situation (income tax rate, property tax for your area, downpayment, etc.)
  2. The add button on the right side of the tool bar lets you add another rental calculator for comparisons.
  3. If you're returning to rentcheck.ninja, save the rental information with save button to save yourself the typing.

    Performance

  1. Leveraging the property (putting down a smaller downpayment) can boost yield and potential for capital gains, but dramatically increases risk.
  2. Finding low cost properties can boost yield as well, but that can decrease potential for capital gains and increased vacancy rates if the area does not support high quality renters.
  3. A one bedroom can earn more per buck than two bedroom. Again, there is greater risk of a higher vacancy rate.
  4. Although harder to manage, properties outside of your area might perform much better (rent relative to price is not always constant).
