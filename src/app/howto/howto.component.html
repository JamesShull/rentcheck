Introduction

 How to use rent check?

Background:

rentcheck lets you evaluate real estate properties as rentals by entering in 4 key values.

  • Price: price of the property
  • Rent: rent you think you can get for the property
  • HOA: home owners association fees due on a monthly basis
  • MelloRoos: Mello Roos, a CA parcel tax, due on a monthly basis
